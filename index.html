Me

I am currently a Senior Program Manager at The Eric and Wendy Schmidt Center for Data Science and Enviroment. I use my background as a biological and computational researcher to lead work that finds solutions to enviromental problems.

Site

This site is an exercise in content availability. As a scientist, I have been very frustrated by the exclusivity of scientific information. I try to work as openly as possible and make freely available as much of my work I can. This website is my attempt to build my skills at conveying content over this vast digital landscape.
