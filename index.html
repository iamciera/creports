Me

I am a Mozilla Open Science Fellow in Michael Eisen's group. I approach much of my research from a molecular perspective, focusing on the evolution and regulation of DNA. Currently working on elucidating constraints acting on enhancer sequence divergence.

I also love to program, especially R, Python, Javascript, and CSS.

Site

This site is an exercise in content availability. As a scientist, I have been very frustrated by the exclusivity of scientific information. This website is my attempt to build my skills at conveying content over this vast digital landscape.

Written in Markdown. Compiled with Jekyll. Framework by Bootstrap. Hosted at Github.
