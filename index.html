Me

I am a PhD candidate studying Plant Biology. Most of my time is spent asking questions and performing research that explores the evolution of plant shape. I approach much of my research from a molecular perspective, especially the evolution and regulation of DNA.

I also am addicted to learning how to program, especially R, Python, Javascript, and CSS.

Site

This site is an exercise in content availability. As a scientist, I have been very frustrated by the exclusivity of scientific information. This website is my attempt to build my skills at conveying content over this vast digital landscape.

Written in Markdown. Compiled with Jekyll. Framework by Bootstrap. Hosted at Github.
